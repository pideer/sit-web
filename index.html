SIT Accounting Services, LLC

SIT Accounting Services began in 2008 with a passion for helping low-income individuals accurately and confidently file their tax returns. What began as a mission to serve individuals in need has grown into a full-service accounting firm supporting businesses, entrepreneurs, and high-income clients with comprehensive tax preparation and financial services.


Over the years, we have remained committed to personalized service, integrity, and financial clarity for every client we serve.
